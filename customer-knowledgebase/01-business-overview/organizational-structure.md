## Decision-Making Structure

### Steering Committees
1. **Executive Leadership Team (ELT)**
   - Frequency: Weekly
   - Members: C-Suite
   - Purpose: Strategic decisions and company direction

2. **Technology Steering Committee**
   - Frequency: Bi-weekly
   - Members: CTO, VP Digital Operations, CFO, key technical leads
   - Purpose: Technology investments and priorities

3. **Operations Review Board**
   - Frequency: Monthly
   - Members: COO, CFO, VP Digital Operations, regional managers
   - Purpose: Operational performance and improvements

## Reporting Lines

### Direct Reports to CEO
- CFO, CTO, COO, CMO

### Direct Reports to CTO
- VP Digital Operations
- Infrastructure & Operations Lead
- Application Development Lead
- Data & Analytics Lead
- Information Security Lead
- IT Support Lead

### Matrix Relationships
- Digital Marketing reports to both CMO and VP Digital Operations
- E-Commerce Operations coordinates with both COO and VP Digital Operations
