# Company Profile - SunStyle Retail

## Company Information

### Legal Entity
- **Registered Name**: SunStyle Retail Corporation
- **Registration Number**: SS-2015-8472-USA
- **Tax ID**: 98-7654321
- **Founded**: March 2015
- **Headquarters**: San Diego, California, USA

### Business Description
SunStyle Retail is a leading specialty retailer focused on premium sunglasses and eyewear accessories. The company operates through multiple channels including physical retail stores, e-commerce platform, and mobile applications.

### Mission Statement
"To empower individuals to express their unique style through premium, quality eyewear while providing exceptional customer service and sustainable business practices."

### Vision Statement
"To become the most trusted and innovative eyewear retailer in North America by 2030, known for our curated collections, personalized service, and commitment to sustainability."

### Core Values
1. **Customer First**: Exceptional service and customer satisfaction
2. **Quality**: Premium products and reliable service
3. **Innovation**: Embracing technology to enhance customer experience
4. **Sustainability**: Environmentally responsible practices
5. **Integrity**: Transparent and ethical business operations

## Market Position

### Target Market
- **Primary Demographic**: Ages 25-55
- **Income Bracket**: $50,000 - $150,000+ annually
- **Psychographic**: Fashion-conscious, quality-oriented consumers
- **Geographic Focus**: Urban and suburban markets in North America

### Product Categories
1. **Premium Sunglasses**: Designer and branded eyewear
2. **Sport Sunglasses**: Performance eyewear for outdoor activities
3. **Prescription Eyewear**: Custom prescription sunglasses
4. **Accessories**: Cases, cleaning kits, straps, and care products
5. **Eco-Friendly Collection**: Sustainable and recycled materials
