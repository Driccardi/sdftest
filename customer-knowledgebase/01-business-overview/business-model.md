# Business Model - SunStyle Retail

## Revenue Streams

### Primary Revenue Sources

#### 1. Direct Product Sales (85% of revenue)
**Premium Sunglasses**: $30M annually
- Designer brands: Ray-Ban, Oakley, Maui Jim, etc.
- Average price point: $250-400
- Margin: 45-50%

**Sport Performance Eyewear**: $6M annually
- Performance brands: Oakley, Smith, Costa Del Mar
- Average price point: $180-300
- Margin: 40-45%

**Prescription Eyewear**: $4.5M annually
- Custom prescription sunglasses
- Average price point: $350-500
- Margin: 50-55%

**Accessories**: $3M annually
- Cases, cleaning kits, straps, replacement parts
- Average price point: $25-75
- Margin: 55-60%

#### 2. Services (10% of revenue)
**Fitting and Consultation Services**: $2.5M annually
- Premium fitting services
- Style consultations
- Custom adjustments

**Prescription Services**: $2M annually
- Eye exams (partnership with optometrists)
- Lens customization
- Lens replacement

#### 3. Extended Services (5% of revenue)
**Extended Warranties**: $1.5M annually
- 2-year extended protection plans
- Accidental damage coverage
- Margin: 80-85%

**Repair Services**: $0.75M annually
- Frame repairs
- Lens replacement
- Adjustment services

## Sales Channels

### Channel Distribution

#### Physical Retail Stores (60% of sales)
- **Store Count**: 25 locations
- **Average Store Size**: 1,200 sq ft
- **Store Types**:
  - Premium Mall Locations: 15 stores
  - Lifestyle Centers: 7 stores
  - Standalone Boutiques: 3 stores
- **Average Store Revenue**: $1.08M annually
- **Foot Traffic**: 450,000+ annual visitors

#### E-Commerce Platform (35% of sales)
- **Website**: www.sunstyleretail.com
- **Traffic**: 2.5M annual unique visitors
- **Conversion Rate**: 3.2%
- **Average Order Value**: $295
- **Features**:
  - Virtual try-on using AR technology
  - Personalized recommendations
  - Easy returns and exchanges
  - Live chat support

#### Mobile Application (5% of sales)
- **Platform**: iOS and Android
- **Downloads**: 125,000+
- **Active Users**: 45,000 monthly
- **Features**:
  - Mobile-exclusive deals
  - Store locator
  - Virtual try-on
  - Order tracking

## Customer Acquisition & Retention

### Acquisition Channels
1. **Digital Marketing** (40% of new customers)
   - Google Ads and shopping campaigns
   - Social media advertising (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
   - Influencer partnerships

2. **Organic Search** (25% of new customers)
   - SEO optimization
   - Content marketing
   - Blog and educational content

3. **Physical Foot Traffic** (20% of new customers)
   - Mall traffic
   - Window displays
   - Local events and partnerships

4. **Referrals** (15% of new customers)
   - Customer referral program
   - Word of mouth
   - Partnership referrals

### Retention Programs

#### Loyalty Program: "SunStyle Rewards"
- **Members**: 65,000 active
- **Structure**: Points-based system
  - 1 point per $1 spent
  - 100 points = $10 reward
  - Exclusive member events
  - Birthday rewards

#### Email Marketing
- **Subscriber Base**: 95,000
- **Frequency**: 2-3 emails per week
- **Types**:
  - Promotional campaigns
  - New product launches
  - Educational content
  - Personalized recommendations

#### Customer Service Excellence
- **Response Time**: < 2 hours (email), < 1 minute (phone)
- **Channels**: Phone, email, live chat, social media
- **Satisfaction Rate**: 95%

## Cost Structure

### Major Cost Categories

#### Cost of Goods Sold (COGS): 50% of revenue
- Product procurement: $20M
- Freight and shipping: $2.25M
- Total COGS: $22.5M

#### Operating Expenses: 35% of revenue
- **Personnel**: $10M (22%)
  - Salaries and wages
  - Benefits and insurance
  - Training and development

- **Real Estate**: $4.5M (10%)
  - Store leases
  - Office space
  - Warehouse facilities

- **Marketing & Advertising**: $2.25M (5%)
  - Digital marketing
  - Traditional advertising
  - Events and sponsorships

- **Technology & Systems**: $1.8M (4%)
  - Software licenses
  - Cloud infrastructure
  - IT support and maintenance

- **Other Operating Costs**: $1.35M (3%)
  - Utilities
  - Insurance
  - Professional services

#### Profit Margin: 15% of revenue
- Operating Profit: $6.75M
- Net Profit (after tax): $5M
