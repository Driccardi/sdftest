## Overview

The Employee Training Program ensures all SunStyle Retail employees have the knowledge and skills needed to perform their roles effectively and deliver exceptional customer service.

## New Employee Onboarding

### Week 1: Company Orientation

**Day 1: Welcome and Introduction** (8 hours)
- Welcome session with HR
- Company history, mission, and values
- Tour of facilities (HQ or store)
- IT setup (laptop, email, phone, accounts)
- Benefits enrollment
- Paperwork completion

**Day 2-3: Company and Product Knowledge** (16 hours)
- SunStyle Retail business model
- Product catalog overview:
  - Sunglasses types and features
  - Frame materials and construction
  - Lens technologies (polarized, UV protection, photochromic)
  - Brand partners (Ray-Ban, Oakley, Maui Jim, etc.)
- Quality and craftsmanship standards
- Sustainability initiatives
- Competitive landscape

**Day 4-5: Systems Training** (16 hours)
- E-commerce platform (if applicable to role)
- Point of Sale (POS) system (retail associates)
- Order Management System (operations team)
- Customer Service Platform (customer service)
- Email and collaboration tools (Google Workspace)
- Time tracking and HR system (BambooHR)

### Week 2: Role-Specific Training

**Retail Store Associates**:
- Customer service excellence
- Sales techniques
- Product demonstrations
- Style consultation
- POS system (hands-on practice)
- Store operations (opening, closing, cash handling)
- Visual merchandising basics

**E-Commerce Operations**:
- Website navigation and features
- Order processing workflows
- Inventory management
- Fulfillment procedures
- Returns and exchanges
- Customer communications

**Customer Service Representatives**:
- Customer service standards
- Phone etiquette
- Email communication
- Live chat best practices
- Ticketing system (Zendesk)
- Handling difficult customers
- Product knowledge deep-dive

**IT and Technical Roles**:
- Technical architecture overview
- Development environment setup
- Code repositories and workflows
- Security policies and procedures
- On-call procedures (if applicable)
- System documentation

## Role-Specific Certifications

### Retail Store Manager Certification

**Prerequisites**: 6 months as assistant manager or equivalent experience

**Curriculum** (40 hours):
- Store operations and management
- Financial management (budgeting, P&L)
- Inventory management
- Visual merchandising
- Staff scheduling and labor management
- Performance management
- Loss prevention
- Store safety and compliance

**Assessment**: Written exam + practical assessment
**Certification**: SunStyle Certified Store Manager

### Customer Service Specialist Certification

**Prerequisites**: 3 months in customer service role

**Curriculum** (20 hours):
- Advanced product knowledge
- Complex issue resolution
- System proficiency (all customer service tools)
- Mentoring and coaching
- Quality assurance

**Assessment**: Skills assessment + customer satisfaction metrics
**Certification**: SunStyle Customer Service Specialist

### Style Consultant Certification

**Prerequisites**: 6 months as retail associate

**Curriculum** (16 hours):
- Face shape analysis
- Frame selection and fitting
- Color and style theory
- Current fashion trends
- Personal shopping techniques
- Prescription eyewear basics

**Assessment**: Practical assessment with mystery shopper
**Certification**: SunStyle Certified Style Consultant

## Training Evaluation

### Kirkpatrick's Four Levels

**Level 1: Reaction**
- Post-training surveys
- Feedback forms
- Measure: Satisfaction with training

**Level 2: Learning**
- Quizzes and assessments
- Skills demonstrations
- Measure: Knowledge gained

**Level 3: Behavior**
- On-the-job observations
- Manager feedback
- Performance metrics
- Measure: Application of learning

**Level 4: Results**
- Business outcomes
- KPIs (sales, customer satisfaction, etc.)
- ROI of training
- Measure: Business impact

## Career Development

### Career Paths

**Retail Track**:
- Sales Associate → Senior Associate → Assistant Manager → Store Manager → District Manager → Regional Manager

**Customer Service Track**:
- Customer Service Rep → Senior Rep → Customer Service Specialist → Team Lead → Supervisor → Manager

**Technical Track**:
- Junior Developer → Developer → Senior Developer → Lead Developer → Principal Developer → Engineering Manager / Architect

**Operations Track**:
- Operations Coordinator → Operations Specialist → Operations Manager → Senior Manager → Director

### Individual Development Plans (IDPs)

**Annual Process**:
1. Self-assessment (employee)
2. Manager assessment
3. Career discussion (employee + manager)
4. Goal setting (skills, competencies, career aspirations)
5. Development plan (training, projects, stretch assignments)
6. Regular check-ins (quarterly)
7. Year-end review

### Tuition Assistance

**Program**:
- Up to $5,000/year for approved courses
- Eligible: Full-time employees after 1 year
- Courses must be relevant to current or future role
- Reimbursement after successful completion (C grade or better)
- Commitment to remain with company for 1 year after completion
