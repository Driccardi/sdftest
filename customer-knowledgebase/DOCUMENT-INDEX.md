## Document Status Definitions

- **Draft**: Document in development, not yet reviewed
- **In Review**: Under review by stakeholders
- **Approved**: Reviewed and approved, active document
- **Archived**: Outdated, replaced by newer version

---

## Document Version Control

All documents follow semantic versioning: **MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH**

- **MAJOR**: Significant changes, structural updates
- **MINOR**: Content additions, moderate updates
- **PATCH**: Minor corrections, clarifications

## How to Use This Knowledgebase

### For Business Users
1. Start with **Business Overview** to understand the company
2. Review **Functional Processes** relevant to your role
3. Consult **Training & Documentation** for user guides

### For Technical Users
1. Review **System Requirements** for project context
2. Study **Integration Landscape** to understand systems
3. Reference **Technical Architecture** for design patterns
4. Follow **Operational Procedures** for support

### For Management
1. Review **Business Overview** for strategic context
2. Monitor **Compliance & Governance** for policy compliance
3. Track **System Requirements** for project alignment
4. Review **Operational Procedures** for process efficiency

### For New Employees
1. Read **Company Profile** and **Business Model**
2. Review **Organizational Structure** to understand teams
3. Study **Functional Processes** for your department
4. Complete **Employee Training Program** for your role
